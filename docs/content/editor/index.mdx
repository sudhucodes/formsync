## Introduction

The FormSync Editor provides a simple, block-based interface for building your forms. You can add various types of blocks, customize their properties and style them to match your brand.

### Key Features

- **Block-Based Editing**: Easily add headings, inputs, text areas and buttons.
- **Real-Time Preview**: See changes instantly as you edit.
- **Custom Styling**: Apply Tailwind CSS classes directly to any block.
- **Responsive Design**: Forms are mobile-friendly by default.

## Getting Started

1. Go to your **Dashboard**.
2. Select a form or create a new one.
3. Click on the **Editor** button to open the visual editor.
4. Click add blocks and click to add them.
5. Click on any block in the canvas to edit its properties.

## Saving Your Form

Once you are happy with your design:

1. Click the **Publish** button in the top right corner.
2. Your changes are instantly live at your form's public URL.
